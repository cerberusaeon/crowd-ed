CROWD.ED

Teachers Influencing Education Policy

Teacher leadership means having a voice in the policies and decisions that affect your students, your daily work, and the shape of your profession. It means sharing in decisions that used to be only made by administrators-- and the best administrators know they'll make better decisions when they listen to teachers.

What is CROWD.ED

Question of the Day

The question of the day allows teachers to provide structured feedback on policies as they see them implemented in schools.

How teachers benefit.

Teachers have a direct pipeline to senior officials and policymakers at the Department.

How DOE benefits

QoD allows the Department of Education to predict impact and determine proper solutions based on reliable evidence.

Sign up!

Policy Planning

Policy problems allows the Department to submit issues to the public that concern them. The intelligence lies in the crowd. Through submission and review of answers to critical policy issues, policymakers and educators will craft solutions that are effective and successful upon initiation.
